What is JaduPC?

JaduPc is a Bangladeshi startup initiative producing low cost computers with ARM based SBCs (single board computers) - running a custom operating system (Shopno OS) based on Linux that resembles Windows. The low cost, dependable JaduPc is tailored for youth education, providing access digital literacy, programming and even robotics. JaduPc aims to arm Bangladeshi youth with the tools for skill building.

JaduPc Computer Lab

Setup Computer labs with JaduPc. We are helping various NGOs and school all around Bangladesh to setup computer labs for school going children. Only 2% of all rural households in Bangladesh have access to computers. Majority of children in rural areas have no access to useful digital literacy. JaduPc hopes to create a major impact in ensuring computer access all around Bangladesh with small school lab setups.
